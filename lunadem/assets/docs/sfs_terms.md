# Shape From Shading: Terms

- `albedo`: reflectivity of the surface
- `photoclinometry`: recovering shape from brightness variations
- `Lambertian`: ideal diffuse reflectance model
- `surface normal`: unit vector perpendicular to the local surface
- `sun azimuth`: clockwise sun direction in the horizontal plane
- `sun elevation`: sun angle above the horizon
- `gradient`: rate of elevation change in x/y
- `regularization`: penalty that keeps the surface physically smooth
- `DEM`: digital elevation model
- `off-nadir`: camera pointing angle away from straight down
- `GSD`: ground sample distance, meters per pixel on the surface
- `roughness`: local elevation variability
- `curvature`: second-order surface bending estimate
